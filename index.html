About the Teacher

Shri Gopal Saha

With over 40 years of dedicated teaching, Shri Gopal Saha has become one of West Bengal's most respected commerce educators. Generations of students have benefited from his patient, systematic approach.

He specialises in the West Bengal Board (WBBSE / WBCHSE) curriculum and Calcutta University B.Com syllabus, ensuring every student excels.

His philosophy: clarity before complexity. Every lesson is structured so commerce feels intuitive, not intimidating.
